About Us

Empowering Africa Through Technology.

PEAREDGE TECHNOLOGIES LTD was established to address technology and educational gaps in Nigeria through accessible ICT solutions, practical STEM education, and intelligent smart systems innovation with a long-term vision for Africa.

Mission

To empower Africa through technology infrastructure, education, and innovation.

Vision

To become one of Africa's leading STEM and intelligent systems ecosystems.

STEM Education

Coding, Robotics & Electronics Training for Africa's Next Generation of Innovators.

PEAREDGE TECHNOLOGIES LTD provides hands-on STEM education programs for kids, teens, and youths through practical learning experiences in coding, robotics, electronics, artificial intelligence, automation, and digital innovation.

Students learn through hands-on projects, practical experiments, teamwork, creative challenges, and real-world innovation activities.

Interactive introduction to coding, robotics, electronics, creativity, and problem-solving through fun hands-on STEM activities and beginner technology projects.
